Howdy!

I'm Carlos. I'm a mechanical engineer.

Interactions with products designed by engineers define daily life. However, I've found that many existing products are excused for having poor designs because they are ordinary. I correct for this in my design practice by applying the same process and standards to every project I take on.

When I'm not CADing, you'll find me rowing, playing computer games, and practicing the bass.
